About

I live and work in Mobile, AL. The son of an embedded systems engineer, I have been surrounded by tech for most of my life. I am interested in using technology to create interesting things and improve communities by bringing added value to whatever enterprise I am associated with.

I received my education and training at the University of South Alabama, earning a BA in Information Technology in 2020. My experience there taught me many things, including the importance of planning and organization in order to ensure a project's success.

I don't shy away from the unknown and so have developed a varied skillset. My focus at university was mobile-first responsive web development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL. Early on in my school career I attempted a startup in which I utilized the WordPress CMS to build websites and plugins, some of which incorporated E-commerce elements. This role also introduced me to API technology.

My internship utilized frontend web development tools, including AJAX, in an MVC framework. I am comfortable with Linux server administration, LAMP stack development, and bash scripting. I have been studying blockchain-specific tools and independent game development since my junior year. This led directly to my role as Lead Game Developer for a Binance Smart Chain-based DAO. I learned C#/Unity in this role, as well as becoming familiar with WebGL. I also learned how to write and deploy smart contracts using the Solidity programming language and worked heavily with third-party SDKs.

I believe that knowledge is power and I have been on an unending quest for it my entire life. I have a passion for learning and am quick to pick up new concepts.
